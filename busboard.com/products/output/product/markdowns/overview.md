Features:

* 830 tie points total: 630 tie-point IC-circuit area plus two 100 tie-point distribution strips providing 4 power rails.
* White ABS plastic body with black printed legend. Color legend on distribution strips.
* Contacts are Phosphor Bronze with Plated Nickel Finish, rated for 50,000 insertions.
* Rated at 36 Volts, 2 Amps.
* Insertion Wire Size is 21 to 26 AWG, 0.016 to 0.028 inches diameter (0.4 to 0.7mm diameter)
* Peelable adhesive tape backing provided for attaching to a surface. Metal back plate provided.
* Size: 6.5 x 2.2 x 0.3in (165.1 x 54.6 x 8.5mm)

![](https://www.busboard.com/images/products/BPS-BB830_dimensions.png)

Description:

BreadBoard-830 is a solderless (plug-in) breadboard with 830 connection tie-points (i.e. 830 wire insertion holes). It has 4 power rails. Solderless breadboards are great for building and testing new circuits because parts can be easily inserted and removed. They are completely re-usable. The BB830 has a 630 tie-point IC-circuit area plus four 50 tie-point power rails. The housing is made of white ABS plastic, with a printed legend giving numbers and letters for columns and rows. The internal contacts are phosphor bronze with a plated nickel finish. A peelable adhesive tape backing is provided for attaching it to a surface and an optional metal back plate is provided. BPS BreadBoards are Lead-Free and RoHS Compliant. Finished projects can be moved to a BR1 or SB830 Solderable PC BreadBoard to make them permanent.



![](https://www.busboard.com/images/genuinedifference_partnum2.jpg)

BusBoard Part # & Website
The BB830, BB400, BB630, BB1460 and BB1660 series now have part numbers and the BusBoard name silscreened on. These names are trademarked so that they are used only on genuine BPS BreadBoards.

![](https://www.busboard.com/images/genuinedifference_rails.jpg)

Red & Black Power Rails
The power rails are now the standard red and black for positive and negative.

![](https://www.busboard.com/images/genuinedifference_contacts.jpg)

High-quality Contacts
Square post connectors can be inserted and removed reliably. The phosphor bronze contacs are specially designed for maximum springiness. It allows both thin 26 AWG wire and large square post headers. The contacts are machine tested for 50,000 insertions to confirm manufacturing quality (human insertions may vary).

![](https://www.busboard.com/images/genuinedifference_grid2.jpg)

Consistent Grid Reference
Grid reference numbers every 5 columns and grid reference lettering in all caps, consistent across our BreadBoard line to provide clear and easy to read hole legends.
