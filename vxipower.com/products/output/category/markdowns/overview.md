### [Oracle DC UPS](https://www.vxipower.com/products/oracle-dc-ups)

[More info](https://www.vxipower.com/products/oracle-dc-ups)

[![](https://www.vxipower.com/assets/catalog/categories/00826d20308bed352963306ff7bfa98a2b96d052.jpg)](https://www.vxipower.com/products/oracle-dc-ups)

**Oracle DC UPS**

This is a range of switch-mode power supplies that feature an on-board micro-controller and a choice of communications interfaces, providing users with the ability to program the unit and carry remote interrogation of a variety of parameters. Primarily aimed at battery-backed mission-critical applications, the Oracle PSU's provides a main output for powering the load, together with a second output for charging an external standby battery.In the event of a mains failure, the battery supplies power to the load via an internal feed-forward diode.

### [AC-DC Linear](https://www.vxipower.com/products/ac-dc-linear)

[More info](https://www.vxipower.com/products/ac-dc-linear)

[![](https://www.vxipower.com/assets/catalog/categories/1bb5fab1d71a6f52b7b70ad82dd5d08fc67a27bb.jpg)](https://www.vxipower.com/products/ac-dc-linear)

**AC - DC Linear**

A comprehensive range of PCB mounting encapsulated power supplies available in either 120Vac or 230Vac input versions, and in single or dual or triple output formats. They are vacuum encapsulated in flame retardant epoxy resin to UL94V0. All units incorporate over-current and reverse protection, and will automatically recover upon removal of the fault condition. All units also include a 'one shot' thermal fuse to protect against excessive over temperature conditions. The units are fully approved to EN60950 and are compliant to Low Voltage Directive.

The Studer inverters have a power range from 0.2kVA to 2.4kVA, for 12, 24 or 48V batteries with a unique battery protection function.
