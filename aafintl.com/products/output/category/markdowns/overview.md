Diesel and gas engines depend on reliable air filtration in both the combustion air intake and engine hall ventilation systems. Clean, filtered air is essential to remove air contamination and protect engines, which in turn improves performance, lowers maintenance costs and reduces excessive temperatures in the engine room.

### Wind Turbine Filtration

Wind turbines are exposed to high levels of air particulate in both onshore and offshore environments. Appropriate levels of air filtration are essential to protect capital equipment, reduce maintenance costs and increase the life of critical components. AAF wind turbine filters have a long filter life to reduce filter changeout, are easy to install and handle and designed to maximize reliability.
